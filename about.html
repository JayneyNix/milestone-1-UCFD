About

Samantha works closely with all her brides to ensure they get the look they want for their wedding day. This involves having an initial meeting where you can chat about any likes and dislikes, what the overall look of the wedding will be, hairstyles, what the bridal party are wearing and also talk about the venue and what kind of lighting there will be for the photos - these are all important considerations before Samantha can recommend what products and techniques to use. Following this, it's recommended to have a trial run around a month prior to the wedding.
